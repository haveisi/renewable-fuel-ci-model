\# Renewable Fuel Carbon Intensity Modeling and Due Diligence Tool



This project is a screening-level renewable fuel carbon intensity model for ethanol, renewable diesel, and dairy manure RNG pathways.



It demonstrates Python-based CI calculation, GREET-style comparison, QA/QC review, and due diligence risk scoring for LCFS, RED, CFR, and ISCC-style market scenarios.



\## Project Purpose



The project was designed to support renewable fuel and biofuel carbon intensity modeling, commercial due diligence, and compliance-market thinking.



\## Fuel Pathways



\- Corn ethanol, Midwest

\- Renewable diesel from used cooking oil

\- Dairy manure RNG



\## Key Outputs



\- Screening-level CI results

\- Python vs GREET-style comparison

\- Due diligence risk flags

\- CI comparison charts

\- Risk scoring chart



\## Important Limitation



This is a portfolio demonstration using synthetic screening data. It is not a verified LCFS, CFR, RED, ISCC, or official GREET submission.
